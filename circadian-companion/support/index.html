Safety Boundary

Circadian Companion is a self-guided wellness rhythm app for local light timing, caffeine timing, sunset wind-down, and tomorrow prep.

It is not medical care, diagnosis, treatment, therapy, emergency support, a UV exposure calculator, a vitamin D calculator, a burn-risk tool, or skin-exposure coaching.

If you have medical questions, unusual symptoms, sleep disorders, medication questions, or sun-sensitivity concerns, contact a qualified healthcare professional.
