A calmer weeknight reset

Shift: Drink Less Alcohol is a private wellness and habit-support app for people who want fewer automatic weeknight drinking moments.

The app focuses on planning a lighter night, starting a guided reset before the usual first-drink window, choosing one concrete action while the moment slows down, and saving a short reflection.

Shift is available for iPhone through the Apple App Store. It is for mindful drinking and habit support, not medical care, therapy, detox support, withdrawal support, addiction treatment, diagnosis, recovery support, or emergency support.
