Safety Boundary

Shift is a self-guided mindful-drinking and weeknight habit-support app. It is not medical care, therapy, detox support, addiction treatment, diagnosis, withdrawal support, recovery support, or emergency support.

If you believe you may be dependent on alcohol, are experiencing withdrawal symptoms, or need urgent help, contact a qualified healthcare professional or emergency services.

SAMHSA's National Helpline offers free, confidential support and referrals in the United States: 1-800-662-HELP (4357).
