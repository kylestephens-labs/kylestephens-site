Shift: Drink Less Alcohol Terms of Use

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Shift: Drink Less Alcohol is a self-guided mindful-drinking and habit-support app that helps you plan lighter weeknights, start a reset before your usual first-drink moment, choose one action, and decide from a clearer place.

Shift is not medical care, therapy, detox support, addiction treatment, diagnosis, withdrawal support, recovery support, or emergency support. If you believe you may be dependent on alcohol, are experiencing withdrawal symptoms, or need urgent help, contact a qualified healthcare professional or emergency services.
